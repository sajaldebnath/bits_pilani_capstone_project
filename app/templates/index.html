Executive Overview

Business Demo Dashboard

This system helps retailers decide which products to prioritize for stocking by combining predicted sales, stocking priority, business rules, and operational risk signals in one guided workflow.

1 Input Describe the product and current business conditions.
2 Prediction Estimate stocking priority, demand, and confidence.
3 Recommendation Translate model outputs into action and caution flags.
4 Insight Show the evidence behind the business recommendation.
